简介¶

Overview¶

雕刻模式 is similar to 编辑模式 in that it is used to alter the shape of a model, but 雕刻模式 uses a very different workflow: instead of dealing with individual elements (vertices, edges, and faces), an area of the model is altered using a brush. In other words, instead of selecting a group of vertices, 雕刻模式 automatically selects vertices based on where the brush is, and modifies them accordingly.

雕刻模式¶

Sculpt mode is selected from the mode menu of the 3D视图 header. Once sculpt mode is activated the Tool Shelf of the 3D视图 will change to sculpt mode specific panels. The panels will be Brush, 纹理, Tool, Symmetry, Stroke, Curve, Appearance, and Options. Also a red circle will appear that follows the location of the cursor in the 3D视图.

Note

To have a predictable brush behavior, apply the scale of your mesh.

Sculpt Menus¶

Tool Menu¶

Here you can select the type of brush preset to use. Reset Brush will return the settings of a brush to its defaults. You can also set Blender to use the current brush for 顶点绘制模式, 权重绘制模式, and 纹理绘制模式 using the toggle buttons.

Hiding and Masking Mesh¶

It is sometimes useful to isolate parts of a mesh to sculpt on. To hide a part of a mesh, press H then click & drag around the part you want to hide. To reveal a hidden part of a mesh, press Shift-H then click & drag around the part you want to reveal. To reveal all hidden parts, just press Alt-H. With the mask brush we can paint a part of the mesh and hide it.

Black part is masked, down in the picture mask/hide menu.
