Manipulators¶

参考

Mode: Object and 编辑模式s
Menu:
Hotkey: Ctrl-Spacebar

In combination with axis locking, the normal 变换 commands (G for Grab, R for Rotation, S for Scale), can be used to manipulate objects along any axis. However, there may be times when these options are not adequate. For example, when you want to translate a single face on a randomly rotated object in a direction perpendicular to the face’s normal. In instances like this, 变换 Manipulators may be useful.

变换 manipulators provide a visual representation of the transform options and allow movement, rotation and scaling along any axis, mode and orientation of the 3D视图. The manipulator can be enabled by clicking on the axis icon from the manipulator options portion of the header or via the shortcut key Ctrl-Spacebar.

There is a separate manipulator for each 变换 Command. Each manipulator can be used separately or in combination with the others. Clicking with Shift-LMB on multiple manipulator icons (arrow, arc, box) will combine manipulator options.

Manipulators can be accessed in the header of the 3D视图:

  • Axis: Enable/disable the manipulators.
  • Arrow: Translation.
  • Arc: Rotation.
  • Box: Scale.
  • 变换 Orientation menu: choice of the transformation orientation.

Manipulator Options.

Manipulator Controls¶

  • Holding down Ctrl constrains the action to set increments. Holding down Shift after you LMB the manipulator handle will constrain the action to smaller increments.
  • Holding down Shift before you LMB click on one of the handles will cause the manipulator action to be performed relative to the other two axes (you can let go of Shift once you have clicked). For example, if you Shift then LMB the Z axis handle of the translate manipulator, movement will occur in the X and Y planes.
  • When in rotate mode, LMB on the white circle (largest circle around the rotation manipulator) will be equivalent to pressing R.
  • When in rotate mode, LMB on the grey circle (small inner circle at the center of the rotation manipulator) will be equivalent to pressing R twice. This will start trackball rotation.

变换 Orientation options.

You can also change the orientation of the 变换 Manipulator to global, local, gimbal, normal or view from the 变换 options menu. The image below shows a cube with the rotation manipulator active in multiple transform orientations. Notice how the manipulator changes depending on the orientation selected (compare A with F).

Similarly, notice how when normal orientation (F and G) is selected the manipulator changes between 物体模式 and 编辑模式. The normal orientation manipulator will also change depending on what is selected in 编辑模式 i.e. the orientation is based on the normal of the selection which will change depending on how many and which faces, edges or vertices are selected.
