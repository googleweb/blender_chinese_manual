3D游标¶

The 3D游标 is simply a point in 3D space which can be used for a number of purposes

Placement¶

There are a few methods to position the 3D cursor.

Direct Placement with the Mouse¶

Positioning the 3D cursor with two orthogonal views.

Using LMB in the 3D area will place the 3D cursor directly under your mouse pointer.

For accuracy you should use two perpendicular orthogonal 3D视图s, i.e. any combination of top Numpad7, front Numpad1 and side Numpad3. That way you can control the positioning along two axes in one view and determine depth in the second view.

To place the 3D游标 on the surface of geometry, enable Cursor Depth in the 用户首选项

Using the Snap Menu¶

The Snap menu Object/Mesh ‣ Snap, Shift-S will allow you to snap the cursor in the following ways:

Cursor to Selected
Snaps the cursor to the center of the current selection.
Cursor to Center
Snaps the cursor to the origin of the scene (location 0,0,0).
Cursor to Grid
Snaps the cursor to the nearest visible grid lines.
Cursor to Active
Snaps the cursor to the active (last selected) object, edge, face or vertex.

The Cursor to Selected option is also affected by the current 轴心点. For example:

  • With the 边界盒中心 轴心点 active, the Cursor to Selected option will snap the 3D cursor to the center of the bounding box surrounding the objects’ centers.
  • When the 质心点 轴心点 is selected, Cursor to Selected will snap the 3D cursor to the median of the object centers.

Numeric Input¶

The 3D游标 panel of the Properties shelf.

The 3D cursor can also be positioned by entering Numeric location values into the 3D cursor panel of the Properties shelf N.
