纹理¶

These are the available texture types:

  • Procedural 纹理

纹理 generated by a mathematical formula.

  • 图像 纹理
  • 视频 纹理

Photos and films projected onto objects.

  • Combined 纹理

Combined textures based on nodes.

  • Volume 纹理

纹理 that can be applied to volumetric data.

  • Ocean 纹理

纹理 generated by an Ocean modifier.
