******************
3D游标作质轴心点
******************

.. admonition:: 参考
   :class: refbox

   | 模式:     物体模式 and 编辑模式
   | 菜单:     Select from the |pivot-icon| icon in the 3D视图 header.
   | 快捷键:   :kbd:`.`


The 3D游标 is the most intuitive of the 轴心点s.
With the 3D游标 selected as the active 轴心点
(from either the *编辑器 Header* or via :kbd:`.`),
simply position the 3D游标 and then do the required transformation. All rotation and
scaling transformations will now be done relative to the location of the 3D游标.
